Qualification Overview

The National Certificate: Animal Production (NQF Level 2) builds upon foundational animal production knowledge to provide intermediate-level skills in livestock management and animal husbandry. This qualification is designed for individuals seeking to advance their competency in animal agriculture.

Students will develop enhanced practical skills in animal care, breeding management, nutrition planning, and agricultural business practices while strengthening their communication and mathematical literacy abilities.

This qualification serves as a progression pathway from NQF Level 1 and prepares learners for more advanced roles in animal production and farm management.
