Qualification Overview

The Automotive Sales Advisor qualification is designed for individuals who want to excel in automotive sales and customer advisory roles. This comprehensive program covers advanced sales techniques, product knowledge, customer relationship management, and the specialized skills needed to succeed in the automotive industry.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this qualification, learners will be able to:

  • Apply advanced automotive sales techniques and strategies
  • Demonstrate comprehensive knowledge of automotive products and features
  • Provide expert customer advisory services throughout the sales process
  • Manage customer relationships and build long-term business partnerships
  • Navigate financing options and insurance products for customers
  • Comply with automotive industry regulations and standards

Career Opportunities

This qualification opens doors to various career opportunities including:

Automotive Sales Consultant
Vehicle Sales Advisor
Automotive Sales Manager
Motor Industry Specialist
Customer Relationship Manager
Fleet Sales Coordinator
